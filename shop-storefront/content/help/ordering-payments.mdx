<Accordion title="How do I place an order?" active={true} >
  Placing an order is simple. Browse our website, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Follow the instructions to enter your shipping details and make a payment to complete the purchase.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What payment methods do you accept?">
  We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay and others. For a complete list of accepted payment methods, refer to our [Purchases and Payment section](terms/terms-and-conditions#5-purchases-and-payment).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is my payment secure?">
  Yes, we use industry-standard encryption protocols to ensure the security of your transactions. Your payment details are processed through a secure, encrypted connection.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I make changes to my order?">
  You can make changes to your order within 1 hour of placing it by contacting our customer support. However, this option is not available if you place the order within 1 hour of 14:00 GMT+2. After the initial 1-hour period, we cannot guarantee that changes can be made, as the order may have already been processed for shipping.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How can I track my order?">
  After your order has been dispatched, you will receive a confirmation email that includes a tracking number, allowing you to monitor your shipment's progress.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I cancel my order?">
  Orders can be canceled within 1 hour of placing them, unless the order is placed within 1 hour of 14:00 GMT+2. After this initial 1-hour period, we cannot guarantee cancellation as the order may have already been processed for shipping. Contact customer support if you wish to cancel your order.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What if I encounter an issue during payment?">
  If you experience any issues while making a payment, please contact our customer support immediately for assistance.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Are there any additional fees?">
  All prices are listed on our website. However, international customers should be aware that additional taxes and duties may apply.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How can I contact you for more information?">
  For more specific questions or concerns regarding ordering and payments, you can reach out to us directly via phone, email, or mail using our contact details.
</Accordion>
