<Accordion title="What is your return policy?" active={true} >
  You may return your purchase for a full refund or exchange within 30 days, provided the item is in new and unused condition. All original tags and labels must be attached. We will cover the return shipping charges. For more details, read our [Returns Policy](/terms/returns-policy).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can custom products be returned?">
  Yes, custom products can be returned for a full refund or exchange within 30 days of the purchase date, as long as they are in new and unused condition. Please note that the processing time for custom product returns may take slightly longer. For more information, refer to our [Returns Policy](/terms/returns-policy).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I initiate a return or exchange?">
  To begin the return or exchange process, email our customer service at [info@eightathletics.com](mailto:info@eightathletics.com) to obtain a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number. Once you have the RMA number, securely package the item with its original packaging and proof of purchase, and mail it to our address. For full instructions, please check our [Returns Policy](/terms/returns-policy).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Who covers the return shipping charges?">
  We will cover or reimburse the return shipping charges for all eligible returns. For more information, see our [Returns Policy](/terms/returns-policy).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How long will it take to process my return or exchange?">
  Please allow at least five business days from the receipt of your returned item for us to process your return or exchange. You will be notified via email once the process is complete.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What if my product is defective or damaged?">
  If you receive a defective or damaged product, please contact us immediately to arrange for a refund or exchange. Check the EXCEPTIONS section in our [Returns Policy](/terms/returns-policy) for more details.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Are there exceptions to the return policy?">
  The return policy mainly excludes items that are not in new and unused condition. For defective or damaged products, special rules apply. To find out more, refer to the EXCEPTIONS section in our [Returns Policy](/terms/returns-policy).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How can I contact you for more information?">
  If you have further questions or need more information about our return policy, feel free to email us at [info@eightathletics.com](mailto:info@eightathletics.com).
</Accordion>
