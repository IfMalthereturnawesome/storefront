<Accordion title="What are your office hours?">

  - **Monday-Friday:** 08:00-20:00 GMT+2 (8:00 AM - 8:00 PM GMT+2)
  - **Weekends:** 10:00-14:00 GMT+2 (10:00 AM - 2:00 PM GMT+2)

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What is the expected waiting time for an email response?">
  The response time for email inquiries varies depending on when you contact us:

  - **During Office Hours:** If you send an email during our regular office hours, you can expect a response within 24 hours.
  - **Outside Office Hours:** If you contact us outside of our regular office hours, please allow 1-2 days for a response.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I file a complaint?">
  The procedure for filing a complaint depends on your location:

  - **California Residents:** If you reside in California and any complaint with us is not satisfactorily resolved, you can contact the Complaint Assistance Unit of the Division of Consumer Services of the California Department of Consumer Affairs in writing or by telephone.

  - **Non-California Residents (U.S. and International):** If you are not a resident of California, whether you're in another U.S. state or anywhere else in the world, you can file a complaint by contacting us directly via email at [info@eightathletics.com](mailto:info@eightathletics.com) or by phone at (+45) 2890 6798.
</Accordion>
