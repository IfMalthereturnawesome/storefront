<Accordion title="What shipping and delivery options do you offer?" active={true} >
  We are pleased to offer free standard shipping on all orders, with no minimum purchase required. To learn more, please visit our [Shipping Policy](/terms/shipping-policy).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do you ship internationally?">
  Yes, we provide international shipping to a variety of countries. Free standard shipping is also available for all international orders. Additional taxes and duties may apply. For a list of countries and more details, please refer to our [Shipping Policy](/terms/shipping-policy).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What are the shipping costs?">
  Shipping is absolutely free for all orders, both domestic and international. For more information, check our [Shipping Policy](/terms/shipping-policy).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="When will my order be shipped?">
  We process and ship orders exclusively on business days. Place your order before 14:00 GMT+2 on a business day, and we'll ship it out the same day. For more details, see our [Shipping Policy](/terms/shipping-policy).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How long will it take to receive my order?">
  While delivery times may vary based on your location, you can generally expect your order to arrive within 2 to 6 business days. If there are delays, we will inform you promptly.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What if my order is delayed?">
  If your order experiences any delays, we will contact you as soon as possible with a revised estimated date for delivery. For more information on your rights, especially for EU and UK consumers, please refer to our [Terms](/terms).
</Accordion>
